Borderlands 3?

I kinda’ feel like it is the back of everyone's mind. Maybe not up there with Titanfall and things like that, but... it's in there. Borderlands 2 was considered one of the most successful titles of 2012, and it seems preposterous for there not to be a third. In our minds when there is a successful title, they make another, right. Well 2K games doesn't seem to feel the same way. In all interviews CEO and co-founder Randy Pitchford has denied they are working on Borderlands 3. He posted on his twitter, “Clean and simple truth: the only Borderlands related game development work going on right now is for Borderlands 2./ it's fun to talk and think but actual effort is focused on Borderlands 2 DLC, Aliens, etc./ Chill out internet”. No one has started working on a sequel to Borderlands 2. :( It is the sad truth. Maybe in the future we will have our Borderlands 3, but in the meantime I have to go kill some Bad Ass Bullymongs and Pyros in plain old number two.

Blizzard is amazing!

Before I get started I just want to say that Blizzard makes by far some of the best games, But I'm not here to be a fanboy I'm here to talk about Diablo 3 and not any normal Diablo 3, Diablo 3 on the playstation 3 and the Xbox 360. This is the first game made by blizzard that came out on a console, and to say it did well would be an understatement. 3.3... MILLION copies sold within the first 24 hours is a pretty pig deal. The game looks great and the controls are very nice and smooth which has been a problem with many RPG's I have played. Diablo is in many regards better on a console, but I think if I'm really feeling Diablo I will still probably play it on the computer because from what I have seen so far it is a much more aggressive game on the PC, but for a beginner I would recommend the Console version. In terms of gameplay it is very much the same except as in most games the graphics look better on the PC. I am not trying to hate on the Console version because it is most likely just my bius keeping me away from it, but I still haven't beaten the game yet so when I do I'll be back with an update on Sean's Game Shack Diablo console.
